About Kalander

Kalander is a dedicated learning centre in George, South Africa. Our highly qualified and experienced team provides individual attention in small groups to develop each child's unique potential.

We believe in a holistic approach to child development through academic support, creative woodwork, music therapy and fun-filled holiday programmes.
