Who We Are

Kalander is a dedicated learning centre located at 104 Meade Street, in the heart of George, South Africa. We offer a safe, supportive and stimulating environment where children can grow, learn and thrive.

Our team consists of highly qualified and experienced educators who are passionate about child development. We believe in the potential of every child and work in small groups to ensure individual attention.

Our Values

The principles that drive us every day.

Individual Attention

Every child is unique. We tailor our approach to match each learner's needs.

Holistic Development

We focus not only on academics, but also on emotional, social and creative growth.

Safe Environment

We create a space where children feel safe to learn, experiment and be themselves.

Quality Education

Our team consists of experienced, qualified educators who strive for excellence.
