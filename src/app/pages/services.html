Our Services

Discover what we offer at Kalander.

Academic Support (Gr 1 - 12)

We offer comprehensive academic support for learners from Grade 1 to 12. Our highly qualified team helps with homework, test and exam preparation, and understanding difficult concepts.

  • Individual attention in small groups
  • All subjects covered
  • Exam preparation
  • Monday to Friday availability

Woodwork Classes

Our woodwork classes give children the opportunity to learn practical skills through creative projects. Under the supervision of experienced instructors, children learn to work with their hands and create their own masterpieces.

  • Practical skill development
  • Creative projects
  • Safe, supervised environment
  • Suitable for different ages

Music Therapy

Our music therapy sessions are designed to help children develop emotionally, socially and cognitively through the power of music. Qualified therapists work one-on-one or in small groups.

  • Emotional and social development
  • Improved concentration
  • Self-expression through music
  • Individual sessions available

Holiday Programme

Our holiday programmes offer a perfect blend of fun and learning. Children enjoy daily activities, playtime and creative projects in a safe, supervised environment while parents have peace of mind.

  • Daily activities and playtime
  • Creative projects
  • Safe environment
  • Available during all school holidays
